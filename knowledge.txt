Diabetes:
HbA1c ≥ 6.5% indicates diabetes.
Prediabetes is between 5.7% and 6.4%.
Lifestyle changes include reducing sugar intake and increasing physical activity.

Hypertension:
Blood pressure above 140/90 mmHg is considered high.
Reduce salt intake, exercise regularly, and manage stress.

BMI:
BMI above 25 is overweight.
BMI above 30 is obese.
Healthy diet and regular exercise are recommended.

Cholesterol:
High LDL increases heart disease risk.
Maintain healthy fats, avoid trans fats, and exercise.
